About stock League


Stock Simulator app that meshes with the fantasy football league concept where users can compete against each other in leagues. Each user chooses their top three stocks regardless of cash amount, and profile points are calculated based on stock growth. At any point, users can choose to trade stocks but can only hold 3 stocks at a time, and their points translate to basis point growth.
